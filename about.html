General Information

Safiri Company Limited is a taxi company that is devoted to giving its customers the safest and most reliable means of travelling between destinations all while maintaining comfort.

The company combines experience, a well-trained work-force and a fleet of well maintained vehicles to unsure the movement of our customers remains flawless.
